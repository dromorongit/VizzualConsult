Course Overview

This comprehensive Tally Accounting Software course equips participants with practical skills to effectively use Tally for accounting, inventory management, and business operations. Learn to maintain books of accounts, generate financial reports, and manage business transactions efficiently.

The course covers all essential Tally features from basic accounting entries to advanced GST compliance, making participants proficient in using Tally for various business accounting needs including taxation, payroll, and inventory management.

Course Details:

Duration: 4 Days (32 hours)
Delivery Modes: Virtual, In-person, Hybrid
Skill Level: Beginner to Intermediate
Group Discount: Available for groups of 5+ participants

Learning Outcomes

Accounting Mastery

Master accounting principles and bookkeeping with Tally

Inventory Management

Manage stock, track inventory, and optimize storage

GST Compliance

Handle GST calculations and compliance reporting

Financial Reporting

Generate comprehensive financial statements and reports

Course Modules

Module 1: Tally Fundamentals

Day 1 - 8 hours
  • Introduction to Tally and its features
  • Company creation and configuration
  • Basic accounting concepts
  • Chart of accounts setup

Module 2: Accounting Transactions

Day 2 - 8 hours
  • Recording accounting transactions
  • Journal entries and ledger posting
  • Cash and bank transactions
  • Contra and journal vouchers

Module 3: Inventory Management

Day 3 - 8 hours
  • Stock item creation and management
  • Inventory vouchers and transactions
  • Stock valuation methods
  • Inventory reports and analysis

Module 4: Advanced Features & Reporting

Day 4 - 8 hours
  • GST compliance and returns
  • Payroll processing
  • Financial statement generation
  • Advanced reporting and analysis
