Course Overview

Professional Excel Training for Financial Professionals

This course is a hands-on practical specially designed for both workers and students studying to earn a career in the field of Accountancy, Auditing, Banking, and Insurance.

The objective of this course is to equip participants with the requisite skills in using advance features of Microsoft Excel for their day-to-day work schedules.

Course Details:

Duration: One Month (3 days a week)
Venue: Virtual, In-person, or Hybrid
Time: To be agreed upon
Delivery Mode: In-person, Virtual (Google Meet)
Special Arrangements: Available for groups of 5+ participants at their preferred venue

Course Modules

Module 1: Excel Fundamentals

Day 1 - 4 hours
  • Excel interface and navigation
  • Basic formulas and functions
  • Data entry and formatting
  • Cell referencing and named ranges

Module 2: Financial Analysis

Day 1 - 4 hours
  • Financial functions (PMT, FV, PV, etc.)
  • Loan and investment calculations
  • Depreciation and asset management
  • Ratio analysis and financial metrics

Module 3: Data Management

Day 2 - 4 hours
  • Sorting and filtering data
  • Data validation and protection
  • Conditional formatting
  • Working with large datasets

Module 4: Advanced Features

Day 2 - 4 hours
  • PivotTables and PivotCharts
  • Advanced charting techniques
  • Macros and automation
  • Scenario analysis and what-if modeling

Module 5: Professional Applications

Day 3 - 4 hours
  • Financial reporting templates
  • Audit working papers
  • Risk assessment models
  • Industry-specific case studies

Module 6: Hands-on Practice

Day 3 - 4 hours
  • Real-world exercises
  • Custom template creation
  • Troubleshooting common issues
  • Best practices and tips
